Help & FAQs

How to register as a farmer?

Go to the registration page, fill in your personal and farm details, and submit the form. You’ll receive a confirmation once approved.

How to view crop tips?

Login to your dashboard and navigate to the 'Crop Tips' section to see personalized suggestions.

Types of soil for crops?

Different crops require different soils. For example, wheat prefers loamy soil, while rice grows best in clayey soil.

Suitable weather for crops?

Weather varies by crop. Use the weather section to check ideal conditions for your region and crop type.

How to enter the crop tips?

Admins can go to the crop management panel and use the 'Add Tip' form to submit new advice.
